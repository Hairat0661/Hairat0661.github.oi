Entry & Exit Travel Requirements for ICRA 2025

Plan your trip to Atlanta with ease!

Entry Requirements

Visa Information: Travelers from Ghana need a B1/B2 visa to enter the USA.

Documents Required: Passport, DS-160 Form, Visa Interview Appointment Confirmation.

Arrival Process: Immigration check at Atlanta Airport. Ensure you have your travel documents ready.

Exit Guidelines

Ensure you have a valid visa and passport for departure.

Follow customs regulations when leaving the USA.
